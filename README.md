# 🎯 Business Objective

Develop an interactive sales dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor commercial performance, evaluate customer and country contributions, identify sales trends, and support business decisions through real-time visual analytics.

---

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- How are total sales performing over time?
- Which countries generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most sales?
- Are sales meeting business targets?
- How many units have been sold?
- What trends can be identified throughout the year?

---

# 🚀 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters by Customer and Date
- KPI cards for Total Sales and Units Sold
- Sales trend over time
- Budget achievement gauge
- Sales by Country ranking
- Customer performance ranking
- Interactive business reporting

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Total Sales** | Total revenue generated |
| **Units Sold** | Total quantity of products sold |
| **Sales Trend** | Revenue evolution over time |
| **Sales by Country** | Revenue distribution by country |
| **Customer Performance** | Top-performing customers |
| **Budget Achievement** | Progress toward sales target |

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Identified the highest-performing countries by sales revenue.
- Ranked customers based on revenue contribution.
- Analyzed sales trends across different periods.
- Monitored sales performance against business targets.
- Enabled interactive exploration of sales data through dynamic filters.
